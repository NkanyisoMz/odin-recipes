Buttermilk-Pancakes


These are flat fluffy cakes made of thin batter and cooked (as on a griddle) on both sides.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour

  • 1 teaspoon salt

  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

  • 1 large egg

  • 1 â…› cups buttermilk

  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. Preheat and lightly grease a large skillet or electric griddle.

  2. Mix flour, salt, and baking soda together in a bowl. Add egg, buttermilk, and butter; stir together lightly, but keep it lumpy. The batter should look thick, spongy, and puffy thick.

  3. Drop 1/3 cup of the batter onto the cooking surface, spreading lightly with the bottom of the cup. Cook until lightly browned on each side, 1 to 2 minutes per side.
